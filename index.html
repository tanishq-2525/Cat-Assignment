About Us

Cat Haven is a community of cat lovers dedicated to celebrating the joy, companionship, and magic that cats bring into our lives. Our mission is to connect people with their perfect feline friends, promote responsible pet ownership, and share knowledge about cat care and well-being.

Whether you’re looking to adopt, learn more about cats, or simply enjoy adorable photos, Cat Haven is your cozy corner on the internet. Our team is passionate about creating a safe and loving environment for every whiskered friend.

  • Events, workshops, and community meetups
  • Support for new and seasoned cat owners
  • Experienced volunteers and cat experts

Care Tips

Caring for your cat is essential for their happiness and health. Here are some tips to help you provide the best life for your feline friend:

  • Give your cat plenty of love, attention, and safe spaces to relax.
  • Groom your cat regularly to keep their coat healthy.
  • Provide fresh water and nutritious food daily.
  • Keep your cat’s litter box clean and accessible.
  • Schedule regular vet checkups and vaccinations.
  • Offer toys and scratching posts for enrichment.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I adopt a cat from Cat Haven?

    Visit our Adoption section, choose your favorite cat, and contact us to start the process. We’ll guide you through every step!

  • What is the adoption fee?

    Our adoption fee varies depending on the cat’s age and medical history. It helps cover vaccinations, microchipping, and vet care.

  • Can I visit the shelter before adopting?

    Yes! We encourage visits so you can meet our cats and find the perfect match. Please schedule an appointment in advance.

  • Are the cats vaccinated and spayed/neutered?

    All our cats are vaccinated and spayed/neutered before adoption to ensure their health and well-being.

  • Do you offer support after adoption?

    Absolutely! We provide guidance, resources, and support for new cat owners to help with the transition.

  • Can I adopt if I have other pets?

    Yes, but we recommend a gradual introduction. Our team can offer tips for a smooth integration with your current pets.

  • What should I bring when adopting a cat?

    Bring a carrier, identification, and proof of address. We’ll provide all necessary paperwork and initial supplies.

  • How do I know which cat is right for me?

    Consider your lifestyle and preferences. Our volunteers can help match you with a cat that fits your home and personality.

  • Do you have kittens available?

    Yes, we often have kittens as well as adult cats. Check our Adoption section for current availability.

  • How can I support Cat Haven?

    You can donate, volunteer, foster, or spread the word! Every bit of support helps us care for more cats in need.
